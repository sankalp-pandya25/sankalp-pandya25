## About

Senior AI engineer specializing in **agentic systems and large language model
applications**. Fifteen years building machine learning and AI in production,
on top of two decades of software and systems engineering.

My focus is the layer where AI becomes useful in the real world: agents that
plan, reason, call tools, and act reliably — backed by retrieval, evaluation,
and infrastructure that holds up under real load. I take systems from prototype
to production: correct, observable, scalable, and cost-aware.
